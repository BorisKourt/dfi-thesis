Periphery is a project that enhances awareness and the lived experience through wearable technology.

\newthought{It is comprised} of a wearable network of three sensor modules that give access to real time and after the fact data about the wearers ambient surroundings. Periphery is also focused on promoting a visual aesthetic for bulkier and more distributed wearables.
